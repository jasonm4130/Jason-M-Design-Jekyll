Do you offer on going support?

We keep in close contact with all of our clients making sure that everything is as expected. We also continue to monitor and update your site for 1 year after launch, with ongoing options after this.

What if I need to make a change?

Small edits to your website will always be done in a timely manner at no extra charge. Larger changes, like adding a new page for that great new product you just launched, will be billed out at an hourly rate for ongoing projects or a fixed price.

When are you available?

We ensure that we are available to assist with any issues with your website when you need us. Having said this we aren't super humans, and would prefer contact between 8am - 5pm Australian Eastern Standard Time. But can figure out times outside these if needed.

What if something goes wrong?

If something with your site goes wrong, and we work really hard to make sure it never does. We will have it fixed as soon as humanly possible. It will become our number one priorioty!

Are your prices fixed?

No our prices aren't fixed. On our pricing page you can get a rough estimate of some of the packages we offer. But we will always work with you to come up with the best solution for your needs at the right price point.

Do I need to pay a deposit?

Yes, unfortunately we do require a deposit at the beginning of each project. This is for both of our benifits. You paying a depoisit helps us as we know that you aren't going to run off on us once we hand over the work. Paying a deposit also helps you, once you have paid your deposit. You are a paying client and as such you get extra special treatment!

Do you have Terms and Conditions?

Yes of course we do, before starting any project we send you a contract, which you agree to and then send back. This is great for both of us as it lays out exactly what we expect from eachother and sets us on the right path together.

What happens when we're done?

After finishing a project we hand over all of the logins and passwords needed just like the keys to a car. We keep a close eye on your project however and make sure everything continues to run smoothly, after all its just as much ours as it is yours.

Mobile vs Responsive

Mobile ready sites simplay display a seperate, usually limied version of a full page to the user. This is ultimately a less enjoyable experience. Responsive design, like the kind we employ in all of our website is different. It allows users to view the same page, but in a way that works great for the mobile devices.

Can you help with my existing site?

Of course, we understand that not every situation calls for a completely new website. Sometimes you might just need a few changes made, or some bugs to be fixed. We can help with all websites.

How long will it take?

Every project is different so timelines will vary greatly. If you have a certain deadline however just let us know (preferably in advance) and we will do our best to have your website up and running by then. On average however a design project takes from 2 weeks to 3 months.

What if I need training?

We work closely with all of our clients to make sure that if they need to know how to do something they can. When helping you we also create detailed instruction manuals, so you can look back on these and jog your memory if you've forgotten something we showed you.
