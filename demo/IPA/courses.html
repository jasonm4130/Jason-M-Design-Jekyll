Study Online

Most IPA students prefer to study online, as it allows them to study from anywhere in Australia. It is flexible learning, meaning you study at your own pace: there are no set days or set hours a week that you need to complete.

Another benefit of studying online is that you are able to work full-time, part-time, casually, and you can plan your study around your work commitments. Stay-at-home parents find this course highly beneficial as they can study while their children are at school.

All you need is reliable internet and access to a computer or laptop. All textbooks are online and you will access recordings and readings through the online systems we provide.

For more information, contact us today on 1800 557 632 or email us at info@iopa.com.au
