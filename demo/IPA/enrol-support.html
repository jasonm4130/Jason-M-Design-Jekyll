SUPPORT

The Institute of Pacific Australia is committed to comprehensively supporting all their students. This is done by:

  • Live Skype calls
  • Regular phone calls, text messages and emails
  • Real time tutoring sessions
  • Tutoring sessions after hours
  • Technical support
  • Academic writing support
  • Career development plans
  • Resume help and development
  • Study development plans
  • Course mentoring
  • Guidance to enrol into university
  • Help to obtain university scholarships
  • Business coaching for students
We encourage all students to touch base with our staff at least once a week. If students need help every day and are willing to put the effort forward, we will have a tutor to work with them every day.
